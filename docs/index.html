Solved Games

The reference for which games are solved — by whom, how strongly, and what’s left. Maintained by someone who actually solves them: the catalog is backed by an open-source retrograde engine that has produced first-published strong solutions (six men’s morris; Y through side 6), with playable perfect-play explorers that solve in your browser.

What makes it different

Section titled “What makes it different”

A real reference, not a list

Every entry states the result, how strongly it was solved, the search-space size, who solved it and when, and the citations — generated from structured data, so the catalog and these pages never drift.

Backed by an engine

A generic retrograde-analysis solver that has produced first-published strong solutions, not just a curated list — with explorers you can play.

The open frontier

Not just what’s solved, but what isn’t — the next targets, what each would cost in time and space, and the methods proposed. A living research agenda.

Corrections welcome

Missing a game, a citation, or a wrong result? Each entry is one YAML file — open an issue or a pull request.
