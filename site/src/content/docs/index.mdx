The reference for **which games are solved** — by whom, how strongly, and what's
left. Maintained by someone who actually solves them: the catalog is backed by an
open-source retrograde engine that has produced first-published strong solutions
(six men's morris; Y through side 6), with playable perfect-play explorers that
solve in your browser.
